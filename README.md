# 💬 Acemagic Community Discussions

Welcome to the central hub for our community! This repository is dedicated to conversations, ideas, and support regarding Acemagic devices and our open-source projects.

### 🎯 What is this for?
Since our other repositories are focused on code and development, we use this space for:
- **General Chat**: Talk about your Acemagic hardware and setups.
- **Feature Requests**: Have an idea for our tools? Tell us here!
- **Q&A & Support**: Need help with a specific configuration or troubleshooting? Ask the community.
- **Showcase**: Share photos of your setup or how you are using our tools.

### 📜 Community Rules
To keep this space helpful and friendly, please:
1. **Be respectful**: No insults or toxic behavior.
2. **Search first**: Check if your question has already been answered in previous discussions.
3. **No affiliation**: Remember that we are a community group; for official hardware warranty issues, you should contact Acemagic support directly.

### ❤️ Support Us
If you enjoy what we are building, consider supporting the project:
- [**Make a donation**](https://buymeacoffee.com/fraank)
- **Contribute**: Help others by answering questions in the [Discussions tab](/discussions).
